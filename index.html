About Me

My Journey With God's Word

Where It Began

Long before "The Potter's Wheel" became a name, it was a lived reality. My own life has passed through seasons of being reshaped — moments of breaking, waiting on the wheel, and being formed again by a Potter who never gives up on His clay. That process is what birthed a deep, unshakeable love for Scripture and a burden to help others recognize God's hand at work in their own becoming.

My Passion for God's Word

I believe the Bible is not simply a book of religious history — it is the living voice of God, able to correct, comfort, and transform anyone who receives it with an open heart. My passion is to teach Scripture in a way that is faithful to its truth, clear enough to apply today, and rich enough to feed a hungry soul.

My Ministry Assignment

I am called to teach, encourage, and equip believers — helping people move from confusion to clarity, from wounded identity to Christ-centered confidence, and from drifting to purposeful living. Whether through teaching, writing, or prayer, my assignment is to help you hear from God clearly and walk with Him closely.

My Vision

I envision a generation of believers who know who they are in Christ, who are anchored in the Word, who pray with confidence, and who live out their God-given purpose boldly — in their homes, workplaces, and communities. The Potter's Wheel exists to help form that generation, one life at a time.
